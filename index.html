We are doing more than you expect

At FoodHub, we’re serving up more than 10+ Cousine. In fact, FoodHub's Famous recipe is one of our unexpected specialties. Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than four decades, is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it’s topped with FoodHub signature herb-garlic butter, then carved tableside.

We believe in the commitment to our community and in fostering long term relationships with local farmers and fishermen. Our menus reflect these connections, featuring local, seasonal produce and sustainably sourced seafood and meats.
